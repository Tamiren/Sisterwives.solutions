Are you happy? Have you thought about changing your life? You might be scared and maybe you think you can't do it. Starting over feels like an impossible dream, but I promise you, you can do it. That's why you're here right? You need, or want help gaining some control of your life. You deserve to be happy. You deserve to feel and know real love, which starts by loving yourself. Find your own truth. Truth shouldn't be forced onto you by somebody else, who was told what to believe by somebody else, who was told what to believe by somebody else....... The world can seem like a scary place outside of the bubble of your own community. This website is a starting point toward building a new life.

This website takes all the information you might be looking for and puts it all in one place. It has Success Stories of women escaping and surviving on their own, if what you are looking for is inspiration. There is also a chatroom so that you can reach out to other women that are in similar situations. On the top right corner of the website is a panic button. Click on that if you want to quickly hide what you're looking at online.

The key to independence is becoming self sufficient through education and employment. The possibilities of where your life can go from there are endless, you just have to be brave and take the first step.
