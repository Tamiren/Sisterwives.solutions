    Employers

  • Walmart

    • After filling out the application online go and introduce yourself to the store manager of whatever Walmart you applied to. It will help you get an interview faster and help them put a face with a name. Be honest and explain your situation. Walmart is a great company to work for, they really appreciate hard working folks. I highly recommend the Walmart in Cedar City. They are always needing help and there is always advancement opportunities there because of the volume, and size of the store, and lack of applicants in the area.
